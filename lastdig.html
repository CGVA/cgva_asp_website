Last Dig in Denver 2008

Last Dig XI is complete! 

Thank you to everyone who helped with and participated in the tournament to make it a HUGE success!  This year’s tournament included 29 teams, 11 of which came from outside of Denver.  Photos from the tournament are available under the “Photos” link or by clicking here.  Results from this year’s tournament can be found in the table below. 

Planning for Last Dig XII (2009) is underway.  We’re looking to make next year’s tournament even bigger and better!  Please check the Last Dig webpage periodically for updates on the tournament dates and other tournament information.  We look forward to seeing you in 2009!

 

John Hausman

Last Dig Tournament Director
