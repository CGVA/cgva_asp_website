The mission of the Colorado Gay Volleyball Association (CGVA) is to provide access to volleyball, player education and social activities for the Colorado GLBT community.

The CGVA vision creates a balanced environment of athletic, educational and social activities. This includes recruiting, coaching and mentoring of players through the organization of its leagues, tournaments, and social events for players. CGVA is open to players of all skill levels and welcomes all participants.
