Welcome to CGVA's

Last Dig in Denver XIII

April 17th & 18th, 2010

The Colorado Gay Volleyball Association is proud to announce our annual NAGVA tournament: Last Dig in Denver!

We have chosen the weekend of April 17th and 18th, 2010 for the tournament this year and hope you can attend.  We will be offering three divisions (B, BB and modified A) and will have a team cap of 24 teams, so register and get your tournament fees in quickly to secure your spot!

More information will be added to this website as it becomes available, so check back often for updates.  Also, here are Team &  Awards Ceremony pictures as well as general social pictures of Last Dig 2009 so you can see what a great time everyone had at the tournament!

We look forward to seeing you at this years tournament for a great weekend of volleyball and camaraderie!

When:

April 17-18, 2010

Divisions:

Modified A, BB, B

Team Cap:

Space is available for 24 teams.  We anticipate a breakout of 9 B, 9 BB, and 6 Modified A teams.  Individual divisions may be capped based on entries received.
