Registration:

We are in the process of finalizing our sanctioning with NAGVA. Once this is completed you will need to register your team on the NAGVA website. We hope to have sanctioning completed and team registration available by January, 25th, 2010.

Fees:

The fee for one team of 7 is $455. It is $65 for each additional player. The deadline for payment is February 29th, 2012. After February 29th, the team fee is $505 until March 31st. After March 31st, the team fee is $555 until the final deadline of April 6th.

Personal checks and cashier's checks will be accepted through February 29, 2012. After that date, payment can only be completed online via PayPal.
