Friday, April 16th, 2010

Registration Party 6 - 10pm

Players will enjoy complimentary Coors Light beer, pick up tournament t-shirt & goodies, meet other tournament players and complete any unfinished registration business.  Late registration will be offered at the gym on Saturday before pool play commences.

Saturday, April 17th, 2010

Seeding Party 8 - 11pm

Find out how teams fared and tournament bracket information! Enjoy Barefoot Wine and Champagne tastings as well as various bar drink specials!  Seeding and tournament brackets will be posted online at the end of this party .

Sunday, April 18th, 2010

Awards Party 6 - 10pm

Cap off your weekend tournament experience with a fun awards dinner celebration at Hamburger Mary's. Players will enjoy a full dinner and complimentary Coors Light beer!
