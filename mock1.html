Last Dig in Denver XII Draws Near!

Registration is open for the CGVA-hosted Last Dig in Denver XII tournament April 18 and 19, 2009.  Teams are already signing up as they know we have a limited number of teams in each of three divisions (Modified A/AA, BB and B).  Get your team signed up today so you’re not left out!

Please visit the Last Dig page for more details on the tournament.  Please continue to visit often as updated information will continue to be posted – i.e. Registration dates and times, Saturday seeding information, Sunday’s end-of-tournament banquet, host hotel, and more!
