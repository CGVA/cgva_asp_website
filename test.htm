IN-DEPTH REVIEW

Once your interview forms have been completed, they then are reviewed both for mathematical accuracy and for the proper of tax theory, as it applies to you. Nothing is taken for granted. We take pride in our work and demand total accuracy in each phase of the preparation of your return.

COMPUTER PROCESSING

Next, your return is processed through a state-of-the-art computer system designed to provide another total review. Not only are the mathematical calculations checked automatically, but a process is initiated that assures you the lowest possible tax computation allowable for your return. The computer then prints the completed information on the required federal and state forms. Your return is assembled in quadruplicate-one set for your federal filing, one set for your state filing (if required), one for your own file and the set for A & R’s confidential file, available if yours ever is misplaced or destroyed.

A FINAL REVIEW

After the computer processing, your return is checked one more time-prior to the preparer signing it-to assure that we have produced a tax return that reflects the lowest tax to which you are entitled-arithmetically correct, technically correct.
